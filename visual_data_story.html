How Music Listening Changes Through Time and Context

Russo Su

Introduction

This visual data story explores my daily music listening habits, including genres, listening time, and common scenarios. The data was collected through daily observational logging using a Google Form. Each row represents a single days observations.

The goal is to understand how listening habits change across days, and what situations influence music choices.

Most Listened Genres

Bar plot showing most listened genres

This plot shows the most common music genres I listened to. Pop and hip-hop dominate, indicating my strong preference for upbeat rhythms during daily routines.

Listening Duration Over Time

Line plot of daily listening duration

This plot reveals that my listening time varies across the week. There are peaks on weekends, likely due to more free time.

Scenarios with Keyword Mentions

Bar plot showing whether music listening scenario mentions keywords

This bar chart shows whether my reported listening scenario includes keywords like study, gym, commute, or work. Study was the most frequent context.

Its interesting to see that on days I listened to music for longer periods, I also reported gym or commute more frequently. This suggests a link between mobility and listening time.
